  1. NMF dimensional rules — confirm bylaw text. Overlay rule was not found in zoning.parquet. Confirm height, stories, setbacks, parking, and inclusionary thresholds at the Town Clerk's office or DPCD's published bylaw.

9.2 Spatial method

Point-in-polygon and polyline-distance queries use Shapely 2.x with WGS-84 coordinates. Distances are reported in feet via the haversine formula on the WGS-84 sphere (sub-1% drift from the ellipsoidal solution at municipal scale).
